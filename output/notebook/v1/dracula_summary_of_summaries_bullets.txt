- Jonathan Harker travels to Transylvania to meet Count Dracula and is unsettled by strange events at the castle. 

- Back in England, Lucy Westenra receives marriage proposals from multiple suitors as she falls ill with a mysterious wasting disease. 

- Dr. Van Helsing struggles to cure Lucy as sinister supernatural events occur in Whitby.

- After Lucy dies, Van Helsing realizes she has become a vampire and convinces the others they must destroy her.

- Mina transcribes and analyzes documents to uncover the truth about Dracula. 

- The men hunt down Dracula's earth boxes to try to destroy him. 

- After Dracula attacks Mina, the group pursues him as he flees England by ship.

- They track Dracula across land and river, finally confronting and killing him after a climactic battle.
