Jonathan Harker travels to Transylvania to meet Count Dracula and is imprisoned in his castle. Dracula travels to England via ship while Harker escapes. In England, Lucy Westenra falls ill after sleepwalking episodes and is courted by suitors. Despite Dr. Seward and Van Helsing’s efforts, Lucy dies and becomes a vampire, forcing the men to kill her again. Mina transcribes Jonathan’s journal and has a strange encounter with Dracula herself. The group tracks Dracula’s movements and destroys his boxes of native soil. Dracula attacks Mina and Renfield before fleeing by ship. Pursuing Dracula back to Transylvania, the men destroy him and the vampires, though Quincey Morris dies heroically. Mina is saved from becoming a vampire herself. Ultimately, Dracula is defeated.
