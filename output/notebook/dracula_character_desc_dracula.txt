Count Dracula is an ancient, mysterious Transylvanian nobleman who resides in a decaying castle in the Carpathian Mountains. He is revealed to be a powerful, cunning vampire with supernatural abilities. Dracula purchases property near London and travels there, bringing death and terror as he begins creating vampires to serve him. He attacks Jonathan Harker, Mina Murray, and Lucy Westenra to gain control over them. Professor Van Helsing recognizes Dracula's evil and leads a group including Jonathan, Mina, Dr. Seward, Arthur Holmwood, and Quincey Morris in hunting the vampire. Dracula returns to Transylvania with the group in pursuit, determined to destroy him.
