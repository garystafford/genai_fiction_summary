
Chapter 1:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English lawyer traveling to Transylvania to help a nobleman with a legal matter. He keeps a journal describing his journey and strange experiences.

2. Count Dracula: A mysterious Transylvanian nobleman who invites Jonathan to his remote castle. He has a commanding presence and unsettling demeanor. 

3. The coach driver: Transports Jonathan through the Borgo Pass. He is impatient and seems eager for Jonathan to reach the castle.

4. The old lady at the hotel: She is distraught about Jonathan's departure and pleads for him to delay his journey, warning of danger on the eve of St. George's Day.


Chapter 2:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English solicitor's clerk who travels to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase an estate in England. He keeps a journal describing strange events and people he encounters.

2. Count Dracula: A mysterious, ancient nobleman who resides in a decaying castle in Transylvania. He plans to move to England and interacts with Jonathan Harker during his stay.

3. Mina: Jonathan Harker's fiancée back in England, mentioned briefly when Jonathan becomes an official solicitor. 

4. Peter Hawkins: Jonathan Harker's employer, a solicitor who sends Jonathan to meet Count Dracula.


Chapter 3:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English lawyer traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase an estate in England. He is trapped in Dracula's castle and fears for his life. 

2. Count Dracula: A mysterious and sinister nobleman who reveals himself to be a vampire. He imprisons Jonathan in his castle.

3. The Three Women: Female vampires who attempt to seduce and bite Jonathan but are stopped by Dracula. They appear to be Dracula's brides or concubines.


Chapter 4:
1. Jonathan Harker: An English solicitor traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase property in England. He is trapped in Dracula's castle and discovers that Dracula is a vampire.

2. Count Dracula: A vampire who resides in a decaying castle in the Carpathian Mountains in Transylvania. He plans to travel to England to spread his vampirism. 

3. Mina: Jonathan Harker's fiancée back in England. He writes letters to update her on his travels and discoveries.

4. Szgany: Local people who sometimes work for Dracula. They bring boxes and other items to the castle.

5. Slovaks: Workers who bring items to the castle in wagons. Dracula uses them for tasks.


Chapter 5:
1. Lucy Westenra: A young woman who receives three marriage proposals in one day. She is torn between accepting the proposals and her love for Arthur Holmwood. 

2. Arthur Holmwood: Lucy's true love. He does not formally propose to Lucy but she mentions her love for him.

3. Dr. John Seward: One of Lucy's suitors. He is nervous but earnest in his proposal. He is noble in accepting Lucy's rejection.

4. Quincey P. Morris: Another of Lucy's suitors from Texas. He proposes in an amusing, lighthearted way using American slang. He is gracious when rejected.


Chapter 6:
1. Mina Murray: The narrator and protagonist, engaged to Jonathan Harker. She is staying in Whitby and corresponding with Lucy.

2. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who is staying in Whitby. She is sleepwalking at night. 

3. Jonathan Harker: Mina's fiancé who is traveling. Mina is worried because she hasn't heard from him.

4. Mr. Swales: An elderly resident of Whitby who tells Mina and Lucy ghost stories about the town and cemetery.

5. Dr. Seward: A doctor overseeing a patient named Renfield at an asylum.
