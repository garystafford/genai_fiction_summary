
Chapter 1:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English lawyer traveling to Transylvania to help a nobleman with a legal matter. He keeps a journal describing his journey.

2. Count Dracula: A mysterious Transylvanian nobleman who invites Jonathan to his castle. He sends a letter welcoming Jonathan to the country.

3. The coach driver: Drives Jonathan through the Borgo Pass in the Carpathian mountains to reach Dracula's castle. He seems uneasy about taking Jonathan there.

4. The old lady at the hotel: She warns Jonathan not to go to Dracula's castle, fearing for his safety. She gives him a crucifix for protection.


Chapter 2:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English solicitor who has traveled to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase an estate in England. He keeps a journal describing his uneasy stay at Dracula's castle.

2. Count Dracula: The mysterious, aristocratic vampire who resides in a decaying castle in the Carpathian Mountains. He is polite but unsettling.

3. The Coachman: One of Dracula's servants who transports Jonathan to the castle. He possesses unnatural strength. 

4. The Old Man: An elderly servant of Dracula's who welcomes Jonathan to the castle. His mannerisms are strange and antiquated.


Chapter 3:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English lawyer traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase an estate in England. He becomes an unwilling prisoner in Dracula's castle.

2. Count Dracula: A mysterious, ancient vampire who resides in a decaying castle in the Carpathian Mountains. He has supernatural powers and entraps Jonathan in his castle.  

3. Three vampire women: Seductive female vampires who try to feed on Jonathan but are stopped by Dracula. They have pale skin, red lips, and sharp teeth.


Chapter 4:
1. Jonathan Harker: An English solicitor traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase property in England. He is trapped in Dracula's castle and slowly realizes the Count is a vampire.

2. Count Dracula: A vampire posing as a nobleman who resides in a decaying castle in the Carpathian Mountains. He plans to travel to England to spread his vampirism. 

3. The Three Women: Female vampires who serve Dracula and wish to feed on Harker.

4. Szgany: Local gypsies who transport boxes of Transylvanian soil to Dracula's castle, which the Count plans to use for his relocation to England.


Chapter 5:
1. Mina Murray: Jonathan Harker's fiancée and Lucy Westenra's friend who is training to be a schoolteacher and stenographer. She is thoughtful and hardworking.

2. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who receives three marriage proposals in one day from Dr. John Seward, Quincey P. Morris, and Arthur Holmwood, although she is in love with Arthur. She is kindhearted but naive.  

3. Arthur Holmwood: Lucy's suitor who she secretly loves. He comes from a wealthy family.

4. Quincey P. Morris: An American from Texas who proposes to Lucy but handles her rejection graciously. He is brave and unselfish.

5. Dr. John Seward: A doctor who runs a lunatic asylum and proposes unsuccessfully to Lucy. He is intelligent and analytical.


Chapter 6:
1. Mina Murray: The protagonist and narrator of the journal entries. She is Lucy's friend who has come to visit her in Whitby. She is thoughtful, observant, and cares deeply for Lucy.

2. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who she is visiting in Whitby. Lucy is sweet, charming, and looking forward to being married soon. However, she has started sleepwalking again, concerning Mina.

3. Mr. Swales: An elderly resident of Whitby who shares local legends and superstitions with Mina. He is skeptical, brusque, but softens towards Mina over time. 

4. Renfield: A patient at the asylum who has an obsession with consuming lives, going from flies to spiders to birds. He is described as a homicidal zoophagous maniac.

5. Dr. Seward: The director of the asylum where Renfield is a patient. He is fascinated by Renfield's madness and theories.


Chapter 7:
1. Mina Murray: A young schoolmistress who keeps a journal chronicling strange events in Whitby. She is thoughtful and perceptive.

2. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who seems to be disturbed by vivid dreams and sleepwalking. She is described as sweet, sensitive and easily upset.

3. The Russian Sea Captain: The deceased captain found tied to the helm of the stranded ship Demeter. His log tells of strange occurrences and disappearing crew.

4. The Large Dog: A mysterious, possibly supernatural dog that comes ashore when the Demeter runs aground and disappears into the darkness.


Chapter 8:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English lawyer traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase property in London. He is Mina's fiancé. 

2. Mina Murray: Jonathan's fiancée and close friend of Lucy Westenra. She is caring for Lucy while Jonathan is away.

3. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who has been sleepwalking and becoming mysteriously ill. She has three suitors interested in marrying her.

4. Dr. John Seward: A doctor and head of an insane asylum near Dracula's English estate. He is one of Lucy's suitors. 

5. Renfield: A patient at Dr. Seward's asylum who eats insects and acts strangely, referring to a mysterious "Master."


Chapter 9:
1. Mina Harker: Jonathan Harker's fiancée and Lucy Westenra's friend who is traveling to meet Jonathan in Budapest. She is caring and devoted to Jonathan. 

2. Jonathan Harker: Mina's fiancé who has suffered some sort of shock or illness that has weakened him. He insists on keeping something secret from Mina to protect her.

3. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who is cheerful in her letters but seems to be becoming ill. She is engaged to Arthur Holmwood.

4. Dr. John Seward: A doctor caring for the mentally ill patient Renfield. He is a friend of Arthur Holmwood. 

5. Abraham Van Helsing: An old professor and doctor summoned by Dr. Seward to provide consultation on Lucy's illness. He is knowledgeable and determined to help.


Chapter 10:
1. Lucy: A young woman who is pale, weak, and suffering from mysterious health issues. Dr. Seward and Van Helsing are trying to treat her through blood transfusions. 

2. Dr. Seward: A doctor caring for Lucy. He performs a blood transfusion on her.

3. Van Helsing: An older doctor and specialist who comes to help treat Lucy. He performs procedures on Lucy and gives instructions for her care.

4. Arthur: Lucy's fiancé. He visits Lucy and donates blood for her transfusion.


Chapter 11:
1. Lucy Westenra: A young woman who seems to be afflicted by a mysterious illness and is being cared for by Dr. Van Helsing and Dr. Seward. She struggles with disturbed sleep and odd events around her home.

2. Dr. Van Helsing: An older doctor and mentor figure who is trying to cure Lucy through strange treatments involving garlic flowers. He is very concerned about her condition.

3. Dr. Seward: Another doctor caring for Lucy. He assists Dr. Van Helsing and keeps a diary documenting Lucy's illness.

4. Mina: Lucy's friend who previously helped care for her. She is not present in this chapter.

5. Arthur: Lucy's fiancé who is also not present in the chapter but she expresses her love for him.


Chapter 12:
1. Dr. Seward: A doctor and friend of Lucy Westenra who helps care for her when she falls ill. He chronicles events in his diary.

2. Van Helsing: An older doctor and mentor figure who comes to help treat Lucy. He is knowledgeable about strange and unusual illnesses. 

3. Lucy Westenra: A young woman who has fallen mysteriously ill after sleepwalking at night. Her condition continues to deteriorate.

4. Arthur Holmwood: Lucy's fiancé who rushes to her side when he hears she is ill. He is distraught by her worsening condition.

5. Quincey Morris: A Texan and friend of the group who wants to help Lucy in any way he can. He stands guard over the house at night.


Chapter 13:
1. Dr. Seward: A doctor and friend of Lucy Westenra who helps care for her when she falls ill. He keeps a diary documenting the strange events surrounding Lucy's illness.

2. Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and expert in obscure diseases who is called in to help treat Lucy. He takes charge of the situation and gives instructions to Dr. Seward and the others. 

3. Arthur Holmwood: Lucy's fiancé who is devastated by her illness and death. He authorizes a blood transfusion from Quincey Morris to try to save Lucy.

4. Quincey Morris: A Texan and friend of Arthur's who volunteers to donate blood to Lucy to try to save her life. He accompanies Arthur after Lucy's death.

5. Jonathan Harker: Mina's husband. He falls ill after seeing a strange man who reminds him of Count Dracula.


Chapter 14:
1. Van Helsing: An old and wise doctor from Amsterdam who is investigating Lucy's death and strange events in England. He is knowledgeable about many scientific and occult topics. 

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan Harker's wife who was friends with Lucy. She provides information to Van Helsing about Lucy's illness and death.  

3. Jonathan Harker: Mina's husband who had a traumatic encounter with Dracula. He is recovering from brain fever.

4. Dr. Seward: A doctor and friend of Lucy, Van Helsing, and Arthur Holmwood. He runs the asylum where Renfield is kept.


Chapter 15:
1. Van Helsing: A doctor and vampire hunter who wants to open Lucy's tomb to confirm she is undead and then kill her to release her soul. He is determined to do what he thinks is right.  

2. Dr. Seward: Van Helsing's former student who is conflicted about violating Lucy's grave. He wavers between belief and skepticism of Van Helsing's claims.

3. Arthur: Lucy's fiancé who is devastated by her death. He is adamantly opposed to desecrating her grave and disturbed by Van Helsing's claims that she is undead. 

4. Quincey Morris: A Texan and Lucy's former suitor. He promises to support Van Helsing even if he does not fully understand his intentions.


Chapter 16:
1. Van Helsing: A doctor and vampire hunter who leads the effort to destroy Dracula and free Lucy. He is wise and determined.  

2. Arthur: Lucy's fiancé who helps try to save her from becoming a vampire. He is brave but heartbroken over losing her.

3. Quincey Morris: A Texan and friend who aids Van Helsing. He is loyal and unfazed by the horror. 

4. Dr. Seward: Lucy's former suitor who assists Van Helsing. He is logical and struggles with grief.


Chapter 17:
1. Jonathan Harker: A lawyer who traveled to Transylvania and encountered Count Dracula. He is Mina's husband.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife who is helping investigate Dracula. She was friends with Lucy.  

3. Dr. Seward: A doctor who cared for Lucy. He runs an asylum and is keeping diaries about strange events.

4. Lord Godalming: Lucy's former fiance who wants to defeat Dracula.


Chapter 18:
1. Renfield: An inmate at the asylum who Dr. Seward is observing. He displays unusual behavior and cravings. 

2. Dr. Van Helsing: A doctor and vampire hunter who is helping the group. He provides information and guidance on dealing with Dracula.

3. Dr. Seward: The head of the asylum. He records observations on Renfield and assists the group. 

4. Jonathan Harker: Mina's husband. He is determined to destroy Dracula.

5. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife. She transcribes and organizes the group's records on Dracula.


Chapter 19:
1. Jonathan Harker: A solicitor who is traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase property in England. He keeps a journal describing his experiences and observations.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife. She is kept in the dark about the supernatural events occurring and the men's work against Dracula. 

3. Count Dracula: A vampire who resides in a castle in Transylvania. He plans to travel to England.

4. Dr. John Seward: A doctor who runs an asylum. He is helping investigate Dracula along with Van Helsing and others. 

5. Professor Abraham Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and expert on vampires and the supernatural. He leads the efforts against Count Dracula.


Chapter 20:
1. Jonathan Harker: A solicitor sent to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase a house in London. He keeps a journal of his strange experiences in Dracula's castle.

2. Count Dracula: A vampire who resides in a castle in Transylvania but purchases an estate in London. He is planning to spread his vampire curse.

3. Dr. Seward: A doctor who runs an insane asylum housing the patient Renfield. He keeps a diary documenting Renfield's strange behavior.

4. Renfield: A patient at Dr. Seward's asylum who has a strange obsession with consuming living creatures.

5. Mina Harker: Jonathan Harker's fiancée who is staying with Lucy Westenra while Jonathan is away.

6. Lucy Westenra: Mina's friend who becomes ill after a strange encounter.


Chapter 21:
1. Jonathan Harker: An English solicitor who was attacked and bitten by Count Dracula. He is weak and ill after the attack.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife. She was also attacked by Dracula and forced to drink his blood. She is distraught and horrified by what happened. 

3. Count Dracula: The vampire who attacked Jonathan and Mina. He is seeking vengeance against those who have tried to thwart his plans.

4. Dr. Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and vampire hunter. He is determined to save Mina and stop Dracula.

5. Dr. Seward: An English doctor and one of Van Helsing's allies against Dracula. He cares for Jonathan and witnesses the attacks.


Chapter 22:
1. Jonathan Harker: A solicitor who is keeping a journal of strange events. He is devoted to his wife Mina.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife. She is brave and determined to fight against Count Dracula. 

3. Abraham Van Helsing: A doctor and vampire hunter who leads the effort against Dracula. He is knowledgeable about vampires.

4. Dr. Seward: A doctor who cares for the insane Renfield and assists Van Helsing.

5. Count Dracula: The vampire antagonist who has been attacking Lucy and others. He occupies the abandoned Piccadilly house.


Chapter 23:
1. Jonathan Harker: A young English solicitor who is traveling to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase property in London. He is overwhelmed with misery due to the horrors he has witnessed. 

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife. She is brave, compassionate, and determined to support her husband. She suggests hypnotizing her to learn more about Dracula's plans.

3. Count Dracula: A vampire who has purchased property in London and is slowly gaining power. He is cunning and determined to escape the group hunting him.

4. Professor Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and metaphysician who is leading the group hunting Dracula. He is knowledgeable about vampires and strategizes how to find and destroy Dracula.

5. Dr. Seward: A doctor and one of the group hunting Dracula. He cares for the mentally ill Renfield who has a connection to Dracula.


Chapter 24:
1. Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and vampire hunter who is leading the effort to defeat Dracula. He is determined and resourceful.

2. Jonathan Harker: A solicitor whose wife Mina was attacked by Dracula. He joins the team hunting Dracula despite his worries about leaving Mina.

3. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife who was attacked by Dracula. She is cursed with a connection to Dracula but resists his influence. 

4. Dr. Seward: A doctor and former student of Van Helsing who runs an asylum. He aids in the hunt for Dracula.


Chapter 25:
1. Jonathan Harker: A lawyer who traveled to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase property in England. He is Mina's husband.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife who was attacked by Dracula. She has a telepathic bond with the Count.  

3. Count Dracula: An ancient vampire trying to move from Transylvania to England. He was defeated once before when he invaded Turkey.

4. Professor Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and expert on vampires who is helping the group try to defeat Dracula.


Chapter 26:
1. Jonathan Harker: An English solicitor traveling to Transylvania. He is keeping a journal of his experiences and observations. Determined to help defeat Dracula.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's wife. She has been afflicted by Dracula in the past but uses her intelligence and skills in organization to analyze clues and develop plans. 

3. Professor Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and expert on vampires. He is leading the effort to track down and destroy Dracula. Wise and courageous.

4. Dr. Seward: A doctor and former student of Van Helsing. Helps try to protect Mina and defeat Dracula. Accompanies Morris on expeditions.

5. Quincey Morris: A wealthy Texan and fearless outdoorsman. Aids the group with supplies, weapons, and transportation needed for the hunt.


Chapter 27:
1. Jonathan Harker: An English solicitor who travels to Transylvania to help Count Dracula purchase a house in England. He ends up imprisoned by Dracula in his castle before escaping.

2. Mina Harker: Jonathan's fiancée and later wife. She is bitten by Dracula, but her soul is saved when the men kill Dracula. 

3. Count Dracula: A vampire who tries to move from Transylvania to England. He is obsessed with Mina and turns her into a vampire before being killed.

4. Abraham Van Helsing: A Dutch doctor and vampire hunter. He leads the effort to find Dracula and kill him.

5. Dr. John Seward: A doctor who runs an insane asylum. He helps the effort against Dracula.

6. Quincey Morris: A Texan cowboy who joins the vampire hunting group. He helps kill Dracula but dies in the process.
