
Chapter 1:
Jonathan Harker - Protagonist: The main character and narrator of the story. He is traveling to meet Count Dracula.

Count Dracula - Antagonist: The vampire that Jonathan is going to meet. He seems sinister and foreboding. 

The Coach Driver - Guide: He transports Jonathan through the Borgo Pass and offers him guidance on the journey.

The Old Lady at the Hotel - Confidant: She expresses concern for Jonathan's safety and gives him a crucifix for protection.

The Peasants - Sidekicks: They make signs to ward off evil and give Jonathan gifts for safety on his journey.
